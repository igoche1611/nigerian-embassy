Ambassador's Welcome

A Message from the Ambassador

On behalf of the President of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, His Excellency Bola Ahmed Tinubu, and the entire staff of the Embassy, I extend a warm welcome to the official website of the Embassy of Nigeria in Prague.

This website is designed to be a comprehensive resource for Nigerian citizens, our Czech friends, and international visitors. Whether you are seeking consular services, exploring business opportunities in Nigeria, or learning more about our rich culture, we are here to assist you.

We are committed to fostering the excellent bilateral relations between Nigeria and the Czech Republic and to providing efficient and courteous service to all. Thank you for visiting.

H.E. Dr. Maureen Tamuno

Ambassador of Nigeria to the Czech Republic
