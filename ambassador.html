Ambassador's Welcome

H.E. Dr. Maureen Tamuno

Ambassador of Nigeria to the Czech Republic

A Warm Welcome to Our Official Website

On behalf of His Excellency, Bola Ahmed Tinubu, GCFR, President of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, and the entire staff of the Embassy, I am delighted to welcome you to the official website of the Embassy of the Federal Republic of Nigeria in Prague, Czech Republic.

This website is designed to serve as a comprehensive resource for information on consular services, trade and investment opportunities, and the rich cultural heritage of Nigeria. We are committed to fostering stronger diplomatic, economic, and cultural ties between Nigeria and the Czech Republic, and this platform is one of the many tools we employ to achieve this goal.

For our fellow Nigerian citizens in the Czech Republic, this website provides essential information on passport services, emergency travel documents, and other consular assistance. We are here to serve you and ensure your welfare. We encourage you to register with the Embassy so we can better assist you when needed.

To our Czech friends and other visitors, we invite you to explore the vast opportunities that Nigeria offers. From its dynamic economy and investment-friendly climate to its vibrant arts, music, and tourism sectors, Nigeria is a land of immense potential. We hope this website will be your gateway to discovering the Giant of Africa.

We are dedicated to providing efficient and courteous service. Your feedback is invaluable to us as we continuously strive to improve our services. Please do not hesitate to contact us with your questions or suggestions.

Thank you for visiting.
