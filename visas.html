Visa Services

The Embassy of Nigeria in Prague provides visa services for foreign nationals who wish to travel to Nigeria. All visa applications and payments are processed through the Nigeria Immigration Service (NIS) website. Applicants must complete the online application form, make the necessary payment, and then book an appointment to submit their documents at the Embassy.

General Visa Requirements

  • Completed online visa application form from the NIS portal.
  • Proof of online payment.
  • Passport valid for at least six months with at least two blank visa pages.
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs.
  • Confirmed flight itinerary.
  • Evidence of sufficient funds.

Types of Visas

Below are some of the common visa types. Additional documents may be required based on the purpose of your visit.

  • Tourist Visa: Requires a letter of invitation from a host in Nigeria or a confirmed hotel reservation.
  • Business Visa: Requires a letter of invitation from a host company in Nigeria, accepting immigration responsibility.
  • Temporary Work Permit (TWP): For experts invited by corporate bodies to provide specialized skilled services. Requires an approval letter from the Comptroller General of Immigration.
  • Official/Diplomatic Visa: For diplomats and officials. Requires a Note Verbale from their Ministry of Foreign Affairs or Diplomatic Mission.

Please visit the official Nigeria Immigration Service website to start your application process.
