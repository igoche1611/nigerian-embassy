About the Embassy

The Embassy of the Federal Republic of Nigeria in Prague is the official diplomatic mission representing Nigeria in the Czech Republic. The Embassy works to strengthen bilateral relations between Nigeria and the Czech Republic, promote economic and cultural ties, and provide services to Nigerian citizens and residents.

Our Mission

To protect the interests of Nigeria and Nigerians in the Czech Republic while promoting cooperation in trade, education, technology, and cultural exchange.

Core Functions

  • Issuance and renewal of Nigerian passports
  • Visa services for travelers to Nigeria
  • Consular services including authentication, notarial services, and welfare support
  • Facilitating diplomatic and bilateral engagements
  • Supporting Nigerian citizens and businesses in the Czech Republic
