Visa Services

Important Notice

All visa applications and payments must be completed online through the Nigeria Immigration Service (NIS) portal. The Embassy does not process payments.

Applying for a Nigerian Visa

The Embassy processes several categories of visas for foreign nationals wishing to travel to Nigeria. Please select the appropriate visa category and ensure you have all the required documents before starting your application on the official NIS portal.

Common Visa Types:

  • Tourist Visa: For tourism, vacation, or visiting friends and family. Requires a letter of invitation and proof of accommodation.
  • Business Visa: For business meetings, conferences, and trade missions. Requires a letter of invitation from a host company in Nigeria.
  • Temporary Work Permit (TWP): For short-term assignments and specialized skills. Requires pre-approval from the Comptroller General of Immigration.
  • Official/Diplomatic Visa: For diplomats and government officials on official duty.

General Requirements:

  1. Completed online application form from the NIS portal.
  2. Passport valid for at least six months with at least two blank visa pages.
  3. Two recent passport-sized photographs.
  4. Proof of online payment.
  5. Flight itinerary and proof of accommodation.

Please note that additional documents may be required depending on the visa category. After submitting your application online, you will be required to book an appointment for biometric capturing at the Embassy.
