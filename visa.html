Visa Information

The Embassy of Nigeria in Prague processes several types of visas for travel to Nigeria. Please read the requirements and instructions carefully before submitting your application.

Tourist Visa

For individuals visiting Nigeria for tourism or leisure. Requires a valid passport, hotel reservation, return ticket, and application form.

Business Visa

For individuals traveling for business-related purposes. Requires an invitation letter from a Nigerian company and proof of business activities.

Temporary Work Permit (TWP)

Issued for short-term employment or contract work. Requires pre-approval from the Nigeria Immigration Service (NIS).

Visa Application Process

  1. Fill out the visa application online at https://portal.immigration.gov.ng
  2. Print the completed form and payment receipt
  3. Submit your documents in person at the Embassy
  4. Track your application status online or by contacting the visa section
