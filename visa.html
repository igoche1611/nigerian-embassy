Visa Information

The Embassy of Nigeria in Prague processes visa applications for individuals wishing to travel to Nigeria for business, tourism, education, or diplomatic purposes.

Types of Visas

  • Tourist Visa: For leisure visits.
  • Business Visa: For business meetings and trade activities.
  • Temporary Work Permit (TWP): For short-term technical or professional work.
  • Subject to Regularization (STR): For long-term employment or residence.
  • Diplomatic/Official Visa: For diplomats and government officials.

Requirements

  • Completed visa application form
  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Two recent passport photographs
  • Proof of travel (flight booking)
  • Invitation letter or hotel reservation (as applicable)
  • Visa payment receipt

How to Apply

Applications must be submitted through the Nigeria Immigration Service portal:
https://portal.immigration.gov.ng

After online submission and payment, print the visa application and visit the Embassy with your documents.
