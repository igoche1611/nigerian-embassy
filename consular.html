Consular Services

The Consular Section of the Embassy of Nigeria in Prague provides essential services to Nigerian citizens and foreign nationals residing in the Czech Republic.

Services Offered

  • Passport Renewal & Reissuance
  • Emergency Travel Certificates
  • Authentication of Documents
  • Police Character Certificate
  • Attestation & Notarial Services
  • Registration of Births, Deaths, and Marriages
  • Welfare Assistance for Nigerians in Distress
