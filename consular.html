Consular Services

The Consular Section of the Embassy provides essential services to Nigerian citizens and foreign nationals. These include passport renewals, emergency travel certificates, authentication of documents, and more.

Passport Renewal

Apply for a new Nigerian passport or renew your expired one. Appointments are required. Visit passport.immigration.gov.ng to begin.

Emergency Travel Certificate (ETC)

ETCs are issued to Nigerians in distress who need to return home urgently. Valid for one-way travel to Nigeria only.

Document Authentication

The Embassy authenticates official documents such as birth certificates, marriage certificates, and academic records.

How to Access Consular Services

  • Schedule an appointment via email or phone
  • Bring all required original documents and photocopies
  • Pay the prescribed fees (in cash or transfer)
  • Allow processing time for authentication or issuance
