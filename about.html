About the Embassy

The Embassy of the Federal Republic of Nigeria in Prague represents the interests and values of Nigeria in the Czech Republic. We are dedicated to strengthening diplomatic, economic, and cultural relations between our two nations.

Our Mission

To promote Nigeria’s image, foster bilateral relations, and provide support to Nigerian citizens living in the Czech Republic.

Our Vision

To be a proactive and responsive mission that advances Nigeria’s interests abroad.

Core Values

Professionalism, Transparency, National Pride, and Excellence in Service Delivery.
