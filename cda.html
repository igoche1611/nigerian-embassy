Charge D' Affairs' Welcome

CDA A.I Mrs. Sarah Bukar-Godwin

Charge D' Affairs of the Federal Republic of Nigeria to the Czech Republic

A Warm Welcome

On behalf of His Excellency, Bola Ahmed Tinubu, GCFR, President of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, and the entire staff of the Embassy, it is my distinct honor to welcome you to the official website of the Embassy of Nigeria in Prague, Czech Republic.

This website is designed to serve as a vital resource for Nigerian citizens, our Czech friends, and the international community. Here, you will find comprehensive information about our consular services, discover the rich cultural heritage and vast investment opportunities in Nigeria, and stay updated on the latest news and events related to our Mission.

Our primary objective is to strengthen the excellent bilateral relations that exist between Nigeria and the Czech Republic. We are committed to fostering deeper ties in trade, investment, culture, and education, while diligently serving the needs of the Nigerian diaspora in this beautiful country.

We strive to provide efficient and courteous consular services. Whether you are applying for a visa, seeking information on passport services, or require any other form of assistance, our dedicated team is here to help. This platform is part of our ongoing effort to make our services more accessible and user-friendly.

I invite you to explore the website and discover the vibrant spirit of Nigeria. We value your feedback and suggestions on how we can improve our services and this digital platform. We look forward to engaging with you.

Thank you for visiting.
