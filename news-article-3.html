Nigeria-Czech Business Forum Announced

Published on: August 15, 2025

In a bid to boost trade and investment between Nigeria and the Czech Republic, the Embassy is pleased to announce the upcoming Nigeria-Czech Business Forum, scheduled to take place in Prague.

This landmark event will bring together key stakeholders, entrepreneurs, and investors from both nations to explore opportunities for collaboration, particularly in the burgeoning sectors of technology, renewable energy, and agriculture. The forum will feature panel discussions, B2B matchmaking sessions, and presentations on the investment climate in Nigeria.

Her Excellency, Ambassador H.E. Dr. Maureen Tamuno, emphasized the importance of the event, stating, "This forum represents a significant step forward in our economic diplomacy. We aim to create a powerful platform for Czech businesses to discover the immense potential of the Nigerian market."

Registration for the event is now open. Interested parties are encouraged to visit the Embassy's website for more information and to secure their participation.
